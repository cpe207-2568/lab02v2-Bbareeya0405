MY PROFILE

EXPERIENCE

REGIONAL COMPETITION

Awarded First Runner-up at the Regional Level in the Innovation Competition under the “Coding Skills Enhancement for Local Applications” project. Our team presented an innovation called Maximum Flood Warning (MFW)

which uses a Microbit and an Ultrasonic Sensor to detect rising water levels in flood-prone areas such as dams and rivers. When the water reaches a preset level, the system sends an alert to help the community prepare in time. I was responsible for coding and wiring the system within the given 2-day timeframe.

NATIONAL COMPETITION

I was awarded 1st Runner-Up at the national level in an innovation competition under a coding skill enhancement training program.

The project involved using Raspberry Pi with sensors, motors, and more. It aimed to solve flooding in Muno community, Narathiwat. I designed the system and integrated data with the hardware.
